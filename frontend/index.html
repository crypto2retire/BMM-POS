A Marketplace Like No Other

Where every booth tells a story.

Bowenstreet Market is more than an antique mall. We're a community of 120 independent vendors, makers, and artisans bringing unique finds to Oshkosh, Wisconsin. From hand-poured candles and locally crafted jewelry to mid-century modern furniture and vintage collectibles, every visit is a new adventure.

Located at 2837 Bowen Street, our 20,000+ square foot space is designed to surprise and delight. Whether you're hunting for a one-of-a-kind gift, decorating your home, or just browsing, you'll find it here.

The Studio

Hands-on creative classes and workshops taught by local artists right here at Bowenstreet Market.

Our in-house studio hosts painting, pottery, crafting workshops, and more. Whether you're a beginner or experienced, our classes are designed to inspire creativity in a welcoming, community-focused setting.

Classes range from one-hour drop-in sessions to multi-week series. Supplies are included in most classes. Check the calendar for upcoming sessions or call to reserve your spot.
