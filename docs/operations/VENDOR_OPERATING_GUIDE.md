# BMM Vendor Operating Guide

This guide is for vendors using the vendor side of BMM-POS.

## Main Vendor Areas

- `Vendor Dashboard`
- `Vendor Items`
- `Booth Showcase`

Depending on your account, you may also see booth-related balances or rent information.

## Logging In

1. Log in with your vendor credentials.
2. If you also have staff access, make sure you enter the correct side of the system.
3. If something is missing, your permissions may not include that feature.

## Vendor Dashboard

Use the dashboard to review your account information and booth-related activity that is available to you.

Common reasons to visit the dashboard:

- check account status
- review booth information
- review balances or rent-related information that is visible on your account
- access other vendor tools

## Vendor Items

Use `Vendor Items` to manage your inventory listings.

Common tasks include:

- add a new item
- edit an existing item
- update pricing
- update item details
- upload or manage item photos
- review which items are active

## Booth Showcase

Use `Booth Showcase` to manage how your booth or vendor presence appears to customers.

Common tasks may include:

- updating showcase content
- reviewing public-facing booth information
- keeping your display information current

## Best Practices for Vendors

- Keep item names and prices accurate.
- Review your item list regularly.
- Remove or update items that should no longer be sold.
- Check photos and item details before saving.
- Log out when you are finished, especially on shared devices.

## If Something Looks Wrong

Ask for help if:

- an item is missing
- pricing looks incorrect
- your booth information does not look right
- a balance or rent amount seems wrong
- you cannot access a page you normally use

## Quick Reference

- `Vendor Dashboard`: account overview
- `Vendor Items`: add and manage inventory
- `Booth Showcase`: manage booth presentation

If you are unsure, stop before making changes and ask store management for help.
