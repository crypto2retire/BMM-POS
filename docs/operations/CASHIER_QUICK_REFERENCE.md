# BMM Cashier Quick Reference

This guide is for day-to-day cashier use of BMM-POS.

## Opening

1. Log in.
2. Open the POS terminal.
3. If prompted, verify the opening drawer amount.
4. Confirm the drawer is `$150` unless management tells you otherwise.
5. If the drawer is off, stop and tell management before starting sales.

## Main POS Tasks

- `Scan or search items` to build the sale.
- Use `Manual Item Entry` if an item cannot be found.
- Use `Held Orders` if you need to pause a transaction.
- Use `Receipt Lookup` to help a customer with a past receipt.
- Use `Orders` to review online orders.
- Use `Gift Cards` to check balances or take gift card payments.

## Basic Sale Flow

1. Add the customer’s items.
2. Confirm the cart is correct.
3. Choose the payment method.
4. Complete the transaction.
5. Provide the receipt if needed.

## Online Orders

- `Pending`: needs action
- `Ready`: prepared and ready
- `Completed`: finished
- `All`: shows every status

Check pending orders regularly during your shift.

## If Something Looks Wrong

- Do not guess on prices or totals.
- Do not complete a sale if an item looks incorrect.
- Ask management before forcing a workaround.
- Use `Held Orders` if you need time to verify a problem.

## End of Day

1. Open `End of Day`.
2. Review totals and payment breakdowns.
3. Count the drawer carefully.
4. Submit the report.
5. Confirm the saved report message appears.

If the numbers do not make sense, stop and get management before submitting.

## Best Practices

- Stay on your own login.
- Log out when stepping away from a shared device.
- Double-check change and payment method before completing a sale.
- Keep the drawer organized during the day.
- Ask for help before adjusting anything unusual.

## Quick Reminders

- `POS Terminal`: ring sales
- `Orders`: online orders
- `Gift Cards`: gift card payments and lookups
- `Receipt Lookup`: find past receipts
- `Held Orders`: pause a sale
- `End of Day`: close out the day

If you are unsure, stop before submitting and ask management.
