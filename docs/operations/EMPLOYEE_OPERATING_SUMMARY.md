# BMM Operating Summary

This is a basic day-to-day reference for employees using BMM-POS. It is meant to help staff know where things live, what each feature is for, and what to do during normal store operations.

## Who Uses What

- `Cashiers` mainly use the POS terminal, online orders, receipts, gift cards, and end-of-day tools.
- `Admins` can use all cashier tools plus vendors, rent, inventory verification, employees, studio, imports, and reports.
- `Vendor users` mainly use their vendor dashboard, vendor items, booth showcase, and vendor-side booth tools.

If a page or button is missing, your account may not have permission for that feature.

## Start of Day

1. Log in to BMM-POS.
2. The first staff member who opens the POS each day should verify the opening drawer amount.
3. Confirm that the register has `$150` unless management has given different instructions for that day.
4. If the drawer amount is not correct, stop and notify management before starting sales.

## POS Terminal

The POS terminal is the main checkout screen for store sales.

### Main POS Features

- `Search or scan items`
  Use the barcode field or search tools to find items for checkout.
- `Manual Item Entry`
  Use this when an item cannot be scanned or is not easily found.
- `Held Orders`
  Use this to park a sale and come back to it later.
- `Receipt Lookup`
  Use this to find and review past receipts.
- `Online Orders`
  Review incoming orders and work them through `Pending`, `Ready`, and `Completed`.
- `Gift Cards`
  Check balances, issue cards if allowed, and take gift card payments.
- `Search by Photo`
  Helpful when an item is hard to identify by name or barcode.
- `Rent Payment`
  Available for staff with rent access to record vendor rent payments.

### Basic Checkout Flow

1. Add items by scan or search.
2. Confirm the cart contents with the customer.
3. Choose the payment method.
4. Complete the payment.
5. Give or send the receipt if needed.

### If Something Looks Wrong at Checkout

- Stop before completing the sale if the price, item, or quantity looks incorrect.
- Ask a manager before overriding anything unusual.
- Use `Held Orders` if you need time to verify a problem without losing the sale.

## Online Orders

The `Orders` area in POS is for online order handling.

- `Pending` means the order still needs action.
- `Ready` means the order has been prepared and is ready for pickup or completion.
- `Completed` means the order has been finished.
- `All` shows every status together.

Staff should check pending orders regularly during the day.

## End of Day

The `End of Day` tool is used to review the day and submit the closing report.

### Closing Flow

1. Open `End of Day` in POS.
2. Review sales totals and payment breakdowns.
3. Count the drawer and confirm the closing numbers.
4. Submit the report.
5. Make sure the saved report confirmation appears.
6. Use the saved report link if you need to review it immediately.

### Important Notes

- End-of-day reports are intended for future review.
- If a report has already been submitted for the day, BMM should show that status instead of creating confusion.
- If totals do not look right, stop and get management before finishing.

## Vendors

The `Vendors` area is mainly for admins and approved staff.

Common tasks include:

- view vendor records
- update vendor information
- reset vendor passwords
- open a vendor's item page
- archive or unarchive vendor accounts when needed

Staff should not change vendor information unless that is part of their job.

## Rent

The `Rent` section is used to review rent status and record rent payments.

Common tasks include:

- check whether a vendor is current or overdue
- record a rent payment
- process card rent payments if enabled
- review vendor rent details and history

If a vendor says they already paid, do not guess. Check the payment history or ask management to verify before recording another payment.

## Inventory Verification

The `Inventory` area is used to review imported inventory and confirm what should stay active.

Common tasks include:

- upload full inventory files
- review items waiting for approval
- approve or reject items
- archive unverified imported items
- permanently delete archived items if you are specifically authorized

This is a sensitive area. If you are unsure, do not bulk approve, bulk reject, archive, or delete.

## Studio

The `Studio` area is used to manage classes and related class information.

Common tasks include:

- create a new class
- edit a class
- upload or remove class images
- delete a class when necessary
- review registrations if your role allows it

## Employees

The `Employees` section is for management use.

Common tasks include:

- view employee accounts
- add a new employee
- assign the correct role
- confirm account status

Only create or change employee accounts when approved by management.

## Data Import

The dashboard may also include import tools for:

- `Import Vendors`
- `Import Inventory`

These tools should only be used by approved staff. Imports can affect many records at once.

## EOD Reports

The `EOD Reports` page is where management can review previously submitted end-of-day reports.

Use this when:

- checking a past closing report
- confirming totals from a previous day
- reviewing whether a report was submitted

## Vendor Users

Vendor users typically work in:

- `Vendor Dashboard`
- `Vendor Items`
- `Booth Showcase`

Vendor-side tasks usually include:

- checking balances or booth status
- managing item listings
- updating booth or showcase content
- reviewing vendor-related activity available to that account

## Best Practices

- Log out when you are done, especially on shared devices.
- Do not use another employee's login.
- Do not guess on balances, rent, or account changes.
- Ask management before using bulk actions, imports, archives, or deletes.
- Double-check the register count at opening and closing.
- Use saved reports and receipt lookup when you need documentation.

## When To Ask for Help

Stop and ask management if:

- the opening drawer is not correct
- sales totals look wrong
- a vendor balance seems incorrect
- a rent payment does not match what the vendor says
- an item should not be sold
- you are about to archive, import, or delete something

## Quick Reference

- `POS Terminal`: daily checkout work
- `Orders`: online orders
- `Gift Cards`: gift card lookup and payment
- `End of Day`: closing report
- `Vendors`: vendor account management
- `Rent`: vendor rent tracking and payment entry
- `Inventory`: import review and verification
- `Studio`: class management
- `Employees`: employee account setup
- `EOD Reports`: review past closing reports

When in doubt, slow down, leave the record unchanged, and ask before submitting.
